The Client

A Growing Retail Business With a Hidden Bottleneck

My client runs a thriving retail business selling CCTV cameras — both wholesale and retail — alongside a dedicated home automation department. Three distinct business lines, each with its own sales team, pricing model, and customer profile.

We kicked off with a digital marketing push — running Meta Ads combined with strategic organic posts. Within just a few weeks, the results were impressive: over 100 fresh leads poured in. The ads were working. The content was landing. Everyone was thrilled.

Until the phone started ringing.

The Problem

100+ Leads, 1 Phone Number, Total Confusion

Here's what nobody anticipated: every single lead was landing on one phone number. Three different people were taking turns picking up that phone — one handling CCTV wholesale inquiries, another for retail customers, and a third for home automation.

🔴 What Was Actually Happening

  • ✕ A wholesale buyer asking about bulk CCTV pricing gets connected to the home automation guy
  • ✕ A homeowner wanting smart lights gets a retail CCTV pitch instead
  • ✕ The same lead gets called back by two different people with conflicting information
  • ✕ Hot leads go cold because the right person never spoke to them in time
  • ✕ Team members are frustrated, pointing fingers, wasting energy

The marketing was generating results. But the system behind it was actively destroying them. Mixed messages, lost trust, slow response times — it was a conversion killer hiding in plain sight.

The Journey

From Mess to System in Three Weeks

Week 1 — Ads Go Live, Leads Pour In

Meta Ads + organic posts start delivering 100+ leads. Excitement all around. But leads are hitting one number with no routing logic.

Week 2 — Chaos Becomes Visible

Wrong people answering wrong leads. Mixed messages. Hot leads going cold. The team is frustrated and conversions are suffering.

Week 2.5 — I Spot the Opportunity

Instead of seeing a problem, I saw a system waiting to be built. Proposed a WhatsApp automation solution to the client.

Week 3 — Bot Goes Live

Built and deployed a WhatsApp bot using Libromi. Two questions, instant routing. Zero confusion from day one.

The Solution

A WhatsApp Bot That Thinks Before It Routes

I saw the mess and thought: this is actually an opportunity. Instead of adding more phone lines or hiring a receptionist, I designed a simple but intelligent WhatsApp automation flow using Libromi.

✦ How the Bot Works

  • ✓ Lead messages the WhatsApp number — either from an ad or organically
  • ✓ Bot greets them instantly and asks Question 1: "What are you looking for?" (CCTV / Home Automation)
  • ✓ Based on their answer, Question 2: "Are you looking for wholesale or retail?" (if CCTV)
  • ✓ Bot instantly routes the conversation to the exact right person in real time
  • ✓ The assigned team member gets the full context — what the lead wants, before they even say hello

The Impact

What Changed After the Bot Went Live

The transformation was immediate. From day one, every lead was automatically qualified and routed to the exact right person. No more confusion, no more crossed wires, no more frustrated team members.

📊 Before vs. After

  • ✓ Zero misrouted leads — every inquiry reaches the right department instantly

  • ✓ Higher conversion rates — leads speak to someone who understands their exact needs from the start
  • ✓ Team morale improved — no more finger-pointing, everyone handles their own department cleanly
  • ✓ Scalable system — as ad spend increases, the bot handles the volume without adding headcount
